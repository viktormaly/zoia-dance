About Me

I'm Zoia, a passionate and experienced dancer specializing in belly dance and contemporary styles. With years of performance experience, I bring grace, energy, and storytelling into every movement. My journey in dance has taken me to various stages, private events, and workshops, where I've shared my love for artistic expression. My goal is to inspire and connect with audiences through the power of dance.

Why Choose Fusion Dance by Zoia?

Every performance I create is more than just dance—it's an immersive experience designed to captivate, inspire, and leave a lasting impression. With a blend of grace, emotion, and precision, I bring movement to life in a way that connects with the audience. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, my goal is to elevate your event with artistry, professionalism, and a touch of magic.
