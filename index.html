Fusion Dance by Zoia

Welcome to Fusion Dance by Zoia, your go-to destination for unforgettable dance performances in the Algarve. Whether you’re planning a wedding, corporate event, private celebration, or simply want to explore dance in a fun and educational setting, I’m here to bring your vision to life through the power of movement.

About Me

I’m Zoia, a passionate and experienced dancer specializing in belly dance and contemporary styles. With years of performance experience, I bring grace, energy, and storytelling into every movement. My journey in dance has taken me to various stages, private events, and workshops, where I’ve shared my love for artistic expression. My goal is to inspire and connect with audiences through the power of dance.
